Backed by Science

Bionic Reading is based on established reading psychology research.

Research shows that readers typically don't read every letter of a word. Instead, our brains recognize words by their shape and key features. Bionic Reading leverages this by creating artificial fixation points, guiding the eye through text more efficiently.

Studies have shown this technique can:

  • Increase reading speed by up to 25%
  • Improve comprehension and retention
  • Reduce eye strain during extended reading sessions
  • Help readers with dyslexia and ADHD focus better

What is Bionic Reading?

Bionic Reading is a reading method that helps you read faster and with better comprehension by guiding your eyes through text using artificial fixation points. It highlights the most important parts of words, allowing your brain to complete the word while reading less.

ZapRead currently supports PDF, TXT, and DOCX file formats. We automatically detect the format and process it accordingly to provide the best bionic reading experience.
